The Story

A Dialogue of
Voice & String

GorJazz is a musical conversation between the mesmerizing vocals of Inbal Hanasab and the virtuosic guitar play of Nadav Ziv.

Specializing in intimate acoustic interpretations of Jazz icons—from Ella Fitzgerald to Billie Holiday—we create a refined atmosphere where every note is carefully chosen to touch the heart.
