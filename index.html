The
Duo

GorJazz is the mesmerizing intersection of Inbal Hanasab's soulful vocals and Nadav Ziv's virtuosic acoustic guitar.

Born from a shared love for the golden era of Jazz, we re-imagine the classics of Ella Fitzgerald, Billie Holiday, and Nina Simone with a modern, minimalist touch that feels both timeless and fresh.
